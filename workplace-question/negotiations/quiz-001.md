- D) Impossible to understand

**7.** What does "ballpark figure" mean?
- A) Baseball statistics
- B) Rough estimate or approximation
- C) Sports venue pricing
- D) Exact calculation

**8.** "Due diligence" refers to:
- A) Being polite
- B) Careful investigation before agreement
- C) Paying on time
- D) Following rules

**9.** What is "mutual benefit"?
