# Team Collaboration Quiz — 團隊協作英文練習題

## Instructions 說明
每題選擇最合適的答案。題目涵蓋團隊協作相關的單字、片語和文法應用。

- B) Combined effort producing greater results
- C) Team energy
- D) Synchronized work

- C) Delay action

**6.** "Accountability" refers to:
- A) Accounting work
- B) Being responsible for results
- C) Account ability
- D) Counting skills

**7.** What is "peer review"?
- A) Looking at peers
- B) Evaluation by colleagues at same level
- C) Peer pressure
- D) Review of peers

**8.** "Knowledge sharing" means:
- A) Sharing knowledge
- B) Distributing information among team members
- C) Knowledge distribution
- D) Shared knowledge

**9.** What does "milestone" mean in projects?
- A) Stone marker
- B) Important achievement or checkpoint
- C) Mile measurement
- D) Distance stone

**10.** "Team dynamics" refers to:
- A) Team movement

- C) Dynamic team
- D) Team power
