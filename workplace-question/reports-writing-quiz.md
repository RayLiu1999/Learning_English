# Reports Writing Quiz — 報告撰寫英文練習題

## Instructions 說明
每題選擇最合適的答案。題目涵蓋報告撰寫相關的單字、片語和文法應用。

**6.** "Variance" refers to:

- C) Variable factors
- D) Variant forms

**7.** What is "ROI" in business reports?
- A) Return on Investment
- B) Royal Institute
- C) Region of Interest
- D) Rate of Income

**8.** "KPI" stands for:
- A) Key Performance Indicator
- B) Keep Performance Improving
- C) Knowledge Process Integration
- D) Key Process Initiative

**9.** What does "correlation" mean?

- A) Project information

## Grammar Questions 文法題 (21-30)

**21.** "The report _____ completed by Friday."
- A) will be
- B) must be
- C) should be
- D) All are correct

**22.** "_____ to the data, sales have increased."
- A) According
- B) Referring
- C) Based
- D) Regarding

**23.** "The findings _____ several important trends."
- A) reveal
- B) reveals
- C) is revealing
- D) are revealing

**24.** "We recommend _____ immediate action."
- A) take
- B) to take
- C) taking
- D) taken

**25.** "The chart _____ that performance has improved."
- A) show
- B) shows
- C) showing
- D) shown

**26.** "_____ analyzing the data, we found..."
- A) After
- B) Before
- C) While
- D) During

**27.** "The results _____ if we had more resources."

- C) will improve
