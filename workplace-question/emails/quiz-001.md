# Emails Quiz — 電子郵件英文練習題

## Instructions 說明
每題選擇最合適的答案。題目涵蓋電子郵件相關的單字、片語和文法應用。

---

## Vocabulary Questions 單字題 (1-10)

**1.** What does "CC" stand for in email?
- A) Carbon Copy
- B) Customer Care
- C) Content Creator
- D) Call Center

**2.** "FYI" means:
- A) For Your Information
- B) For Your Interest
- C) Find Your Item
- D) Fix Your Issue

**3.** What is the "subject line" of an email?
- A) The main content
- B) The recipient's name
- C) The title/header describing the email content
- D) The sender's signature

**4.** "To escalate" an issue means to:
- A) Make it worse
- B) Raise it to a higher level
- C) Solve it quickly
- D) Ignore it

**5.** What does "urgent" mean?
- A) Not important
- B) Requiring immediate attention
- C) Can wait
- D) Optional

**6.** An "attachment" is:
- A) A feeling of connection
- B) A file sent with an email
- C) The email address
- D) The email footer

**7.** "To acknowledge" means:
- A) To ignore
- B) To confirm receipt or understanding
- C) To forward
- D) To delete

**8.** What is a "thread" in email context?
- A) A sewing material
- B) A series of related email messages
- C) An email attachment
- D) The email signature

**9.** "ETA" stands for:
- A) Estimated Time of Arrival
- B) Electronic Transfer Authorization
- C) Email Traffic Analysis
- D) Emergency Task Assignment

**10.** "To expedite" means:
- A) To delay
- B) To cancel
- C) To speed up or accelerate
- D) To repeat
