# Incident Management Quiz — 事故處理英文練習題

## Instructions 說明
每題選擇最合適的答案。題目涵蓋事故處理相關的單字、片語和文法應用。

## Vocabulary Questions 單字題 (1-10)

**1.** What does "Sev-1" mean in incident management?
- A) Seven incidents
- B) Severity level 1 (highest priority)
- C) Server 1
- D) Service 1

**2.** "MTTR" stands for:
- A) Mean Time To Recovery
- B) Maximum Time To Repair
- C) Minimum Time To Response
- D) Monthly Technical Time Report

**3.** What is a "postmortem"?
- A) After death examination
- B) Analysis conducted after incident resolution
- C) Post meeting
- D) Morning post

**4.** "Root cause" refers to:
- A) Plant roots
- B) Fundamental reason for the incident
- C) Cause at the root
- D) Basic cause

**5.** What does "escalate" mean in incident context?
- A) Use escalator
- B) Raise issue to higher authority level
- C) Make worse
- D) Increase scale

**6.** "SLA" stands for:
- A) Service Level Agreement
- B) System Load Analysis
- C) Software License Authorization
- D) Standard Legal Agreement

**7.** What is "downtime"?
- A) Time to go down
- B) Period when service is unavailable
- C) Down time
- D) Afternoon time

**8.** "Rollback" means:
- A) Roll back physically
- B) Revert to previous working version
- C) Roll backwards
- D) Back rolling

**9.** What does "impact" mean in incidents?
- A) Physical collision
- B) Effect or consequences of the incident
- C) Impacting force
- D) Impact measurement

**10.** "War room" refers to:
- A) Room for wars
- B) Command center for incident response
- C) Military room
- D) War game room

- D) will respond
