# Interviews Quiz — 面試英文練習題

## Instructions 說明
每題選擇最合適的答案。題目涵蓋面試相關的單字、片語和文法應用。

## Vocabulary Questions 單字題 (1-10)

**1.** What does "qualifications" mean?
- A) Quality control
- B) Skills, education, and experience for a job
- C) Job requirements
- D) Personality traits

**2.** A "candidate" is:
- A) Someone running for office
- B) A person applying for a job
- C) An interviewer
- D) A sweet food

**3.** What does "compensation" refer to?
- A) Making up for mistakes
- B) Salary and benefits package
- C) Competition
- D) Compliments

**4.** "Professional development" means:
- A) Photo development
- B) Improving work-related skills and knowledge

**5.** What is "work-life balance"?

- B) Equal distribution of time between work and personal life

**6.** "Career progression" refers to:
- A) Walking forward
- B) Advancement in one's career
- C) Career change
- D) Mathematical progression

**7.** What does "initiative" mean in a work context?
- A) Starting something
- B) Taking action without being told
- C) A new project
- D) All of the above

**8.** "Multitasking" means:
- A) Having multiple jobs
- B) Doing several tasks simultaneously
- C) Working overtime
- D) Team collaboration

**9.** What is "onboarding"?
- A) Getting on a ship
- B) The process of integrating new employees
- C) Using a computer
- D) Board meetings

**10.** "Leadership" refers to:
- A) Being the boss
- B) The ability to guide and inspire others
- C) Leading a meeting
- D) Walking in front
