# Presentations Quiz — 簡報演示英文練習題

## Instructions 說明
每題選擇最合適的答案。題目涵蓋簡報相關的單字、片語和文法應用。

## Vocabulary Questions 單字題 (1-10)

**1.** What is the "outline" of a presentation?
- A) The border of slides
- B) The structure or main points
- C) The presentation time
- D) The speaker's notes

**2.** A "slide deck" refers to:
- A) A stack of papers
- B) A collection of presentation slides
- C) The presentation stage
- D) Audio equipment

**3.** What does "engaging" mean in presentation context?
- A) Getting married
- B) Interesting and holding attention
- C) Starting the presentation
- D) Ending the presentation

**4.** "Visual aids" are:
- A) Eye glasses
- B) Charts, graphs, images that support the presentation
- C) Lighting equipment
- D) Stage decorations

**5.** What is a "handout"?
- A) Money given away
- B) Printed materials given to audience
- C) Hand gestures
- D) Helping someone

**6.** "To elaborate" means:
- A) To make something fancy
- B) To provide more detailed explanation
- C) To make it shorter
- D) To repeat exactly

**7.** What does "compelling" mean?
- A) Forcing someone
- B) Convincing and persuasive
- C) Completing something
- D) Comparing things

**8.** A "takeaway" from a presentation is:
- A) Food to go
- B) Key message or lesson learned
- C) Something removed
- D) The conclusion slide

**9.** "Rapport" with audience means:
- A) A written report
- B) Good connection and understanding
- C) Repetition
- D) Rapid speaking

**10.** What does "articulate" mean?
- A) Having joints
- B) Speaking clearly and effectively
- C) Using art in presentation
- D) Moving your hands

## Grammar Questions 文法題 (21-30)

**21.** "Today I _____ be talking about our new strategy."
- A) will
- B) am going to
- C) shall
- D) All are correct

**22.** "_____ you can see from this chart, sales have increased."
- A) As
- B) Like
- C) Such as
- D) Similar to

**23.** "This data suggests that we _____ invest more in marketing."
- A) should
- B) would
- C) could
- D) might

**24.** "_____ I mentioned earlier, this is a critical issue."
- A) As
- B) Like
- C) Such as
- D) Same as

**25.** "The results speak _____ themselves."
- A) for
- B) by
- C) to
- D) with

**26.** "Let me _____ you a question."
- A) make
- B) ask
- C) give
- D) tell

**27.** "This graph _____ the trend over the past year."
- A) is showing
- B) shows
- C) has shown
- D) All are correct

**28.** "If we _____ this strategy, we would see better results."
- A) implement
- B) implemented
- C) will implement
- D) have implemented

**29.** "_____ any questions so far?"
- A) Are there
- B) Do you have
- C) Is there
- D) Both A and B

**30.** "Thank you for _____ attention."
- A) your
- B) you're
- C) yours
- D) yourself
