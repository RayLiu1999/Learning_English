# Meetings Quiz — 會議英文練習題

## Instructions 說明
每題選擇最合適的答案。題目涵蓋會議相關的單字、片語和文法應用。

---

## Vocabulary Questions 單字題 (1-10)

**1.** What does "agenda" mean in a meeting context?
- A) The meeting room
- B) A list of topics to be discussed
- C) The meeting duration
- D) The meeting participants

**2.** Which word means "to discuss something thoroughly"?
- A) skim
- B) glance
- C) deliberate
- D) browse

**3.** What is a "stakeholder"?
- A) Someone who takes notes
- B) A person with an interest in the project
- C) The meeting facilitator
- D) A time keeper

**4.** "Let's table this discussion" means:
- A) Put it on the table
- B) Postpone it for later
- C) Make it urgent
- D) Write it down

**5.** What does "actionable" mean?
- A) Can be acted upon
- B) Very active
- C) Needs action immediately
- D) Cannot be done

**6.** A "deliverable" refers to:
- A) Mail service
- B) Something that can be delivered
- C) A tangible outcome or result
- D) Delivery schedule

**7.** What does "bandwidth" mean in business context?
- A) Internet speed
- B) Available capacity or time
- C) Radio frequency
- D) Meeting room size

**8.** "To sync up" means:
- A) To synchronize schedules
- B) To coordinate or align
- C) To backup data
- D) To meet in person

**9.** What is a "retrospective"?
- A) Looking backward at past performance
- B) A future prediction
- C) A type of meeting room
- D) A presentation style

**10.** "Buy-in" refers to:
- A) Purchasing something
- B) Agreement and support
- C) Investment amount
- D) Entry fee
